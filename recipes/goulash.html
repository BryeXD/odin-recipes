Grandma's Goulash

Description

An American style goulash that tastes like a warm hug on a cold day! A a beefy mix of onion, green bell peppers, garlic, and tomatoes will surely satisfy any craving you may have.

Servings : 10 Servings

Estimated time : 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 cup of onion (diced)
  • 1 green bell pepper (diced)
  • 2 pounds of ground beef (80/20 mix if possible)
  • 3 teaspoons of garlic (minced)
  • 2, 15 ounce cans of tomato sauce
  • 2, 15 ounce cans of petite diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups of beef broth
  • 3 tablespoons of Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 teaspoons of seasoned salt
  • 2 tablespoons of Italian Seasoning
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 cups of macaroni noodles (dried)
  • Chedder cheese (shredded)
  • Sour cream (optional)
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Equipment

  • A large pot (preferably a dutch oven)
  • Wooden spoon

Recipe

  1. Add olive oil to pot over medium high heat.
  2. Add the onion, bell pepper, and ground beef then cook until beef is no longer pink
  3. Drain any excess fat and return pot to stove top.
  4. Add in garlic and stir until fragrant (30 seconds).
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, beef broth, Worcestershire Sauce, seasoned salt, Italian Seasoning, bay leaves, and dried macaroni noodles.
  6. Stir and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat to a light boil and stir occasionally until pasta is tender (20 minutes).
  8. Remove bay leaves and take off heat.
  9. Portion out into a bowl and top with cheddar cheese, sour cream, and hot sauce.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
