Homemade White Bread

Description

A delicous white bread perfect for sandwiches and just about everything else!

Ingredients

  • Seven cups of bread flour
  • Three cups of water
  • Two tablespoons of instant yeast (two packets)
  • Three teaspoons of salt
  • Two tablespoons of oil (vegetable, canola, and olive oil will all work fine)
  • Two tablespoons of salted butter
  • A stick of salted butter

Equipment

  • Oven
  • Standmixer with dough hook (optional but highly recommended)
  • Mixing bowl
  • Two loaf pans
  • Microwave
  • Silicone basting brush

Recipe

Yields two loaves of bread

Estimated time : 3 hours

  1. Add lukewarm water to a mixing bowl.
  2. Add yeast to the water and mix until yeast dissolves.
  3. Let sit for a few minutes.
  4. Add flour, salt, and oil to the mixing bowl.
  5. Mix until it is a doughy consistency (use a standmixer on low with dough hook if you have one).
  6. Turn dough onto a well floured board and knead for eight minutes (this step can get messy as the dough will stick to your hands).
  7. Form dough into a ball shape, cover with a clean towel, and let rest for 3 minutes.
  8. Turn the ball over and flatten it out.
  9. Start to shape the dough into a ball by grabbing an end, and folding it over.
  10. Sprinkle flour on top of the ball of dough, cover again with a clean towel, and let rest for one hour.
  11. Remove towel and flatten dough ball into a pancake like shape. Cut down the middle to make two even pieces of dough.
  12. Preshape your loaves by folding one end of the dough almost halfway lengthwise, and the other end completely over it.
  13. Knead until it is in a ball shape. Sprinkle a pinch of flour on top when done.
  14. Repeat for other half of the dough.
  15. Cover both with a clean towel and let rest for 15 minutes.
  16. While it rests, grease your loaf pans with a stick of butter.
  17. Flatten the ball of dough and fold one end at an angle.
  18. Fold opposite end over the just folded end. It should be a triangle like shape.
  19. Take smallest end and roll it over itself into a loaf-like shape. Pinch the end into the loaf so it is seamless.
  20. Rub a sprinkle of flour onto the dough until it is evenly covered.
  21. Place the dough into the loaf pan.
  22. Repeat for other dough piece.
  23. Cover both loaf pans with a clean towel and let rest for one hour.
  24. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  25. Bake for 40 minutes, but start checking at 35 in case they finish early.
  26. Take bread out of oven and transfer onto wire rack.
  27. Brush top of bread with melted butter from microwave.
  28. For best results, don't cut into the bread until the next day.
