Copycat Texas Roadhouse Butter

Description

Have you ever went to Texas Roadhouse just for the butter they serve you when you first order? Now you can have it at home whenever you want!

Yield : 8 Tablespoons

Estimated Time : 5 Minutes

Ingredients

  • One stick of salted butter (at room temperature).
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoons of honey
  • 3/4 teaspoon of cinnamon

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • A rubber spatula

Recipe

  1. Ensure butter is very soft and has been at room tempature for at least an hour.
  2. Mix all ingredients until its creamy and smooth
  3. Serve and enjoy!
