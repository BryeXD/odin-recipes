Chicken Noodle Soup from Scratch

Description

A yummy soup that will have you screaming for seconds! The dill really rounds out the flavor in a way that other recipes cannot encapsulate.

Yield : 6-8 Servings

Estimated time : 1 hour and 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 2 carrots (diced)
  • 2 celery ribs (diced)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 8 cups chicken stock
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • 2 and 1/2 pounds bone-in, skinless chicken breasts (other cuts work as well, just make sure it is skinless)
  • 2 and 1/2 cups wide egg noodles
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice, or more to taste

Equipment

  • A large stock pot (Dutch oven works best)
  • A wooden spoon

Recipe

  1. Melt butter in a large stock pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, carrot, and celery. Cook, stirring occasionally, until tender (about 3-4 minutes).
  3. Stir in garlic until fragrant (about one minute).
  4. Stir in chicken stock and bay leaves; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Add chicken and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until chicken is cooked through (about 30-40 minutes).
  6. Remove chicken and let cool before shredding into bite size pieces, discarding bones.
  7. Stir in chicken and pasta and cook until tender (about 6-7 minutes).
  8. Remove from heat; stir in parsley, dill and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
